Overview

Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data from users of the apps and services covered by this policy.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising tools, tracking pixels, or other behavioral monitoring systems in the apps covered by this policy.

On Device

Storage

If an app lets you create or save content locally, that content remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

KSHR does not receive that content and cannot access it through our services.

Their practices are governed by their privacy policies, not this one.

Because we do not collect personal information, we also do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
