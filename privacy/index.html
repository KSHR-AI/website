Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you.

This policy applies to apps, websites, and related services published by KSHR unless a product includes its own separate privacy policy.

Effective date: April 23, 2026

What we collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data from our users.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or other behavioral monitoring tools in the apps covered by this policy.

Accounts and sign-in

Our apps do not require user accounts, and we do not maintain user profiles or cloud-hosted personal records.

Data stored on your device

If an app lets you create or save content locally, that information remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself. KSHR does not receive that content.

Third parties

Platform providers such as Apple, Google, or your browser vendor may collect information under their own terms. Their data practices are governed by their privacy policies, not ours.

Children's privacy

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices change, we will update this page before those changes take effect.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@kshr.ai.
